<p>We had planned to make an autonomous ground vehicle(AGV) which moves in a particular direction and avoids all the obstacles in its pathway . The basic idea of the project was to make a robot vehicle which will detect all the obstacles in its pathway and avoids them very smartly.We used an ultrasonic sensor and an imu along with pid controller to accomplish this.</p><p><a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/11/FCS-MULE-ARV-2007.jpg"></a></p><p><img src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/11/FCS-MULE-ARV-2007.jpg"></p>
